To Choice Charities

Where we help you, help make a difference.

We are a small not for profit organisation with a big dream. We want to provide a platform that will enable users to find a wide variety of charity works, conservation efforts, community projects or other crowd funded projects from all across Australia.

Donating to charity shouldn't be difficult or time consuming, and thats why we made Choice Charities.
